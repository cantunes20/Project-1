HILLARY CLINTON

I'm with her; Stronger together

Hillary Clinton is the Democratic Nominee for the upcoming election. She was elected to the Senate in 2001, she became the Secretary of State in 2009, and in 2016, she became the first woman to become a presidential nominee of major political party. (Biography.com)

As of August 31st, Clinton has raised $759 million dollars for her campaign. Most of the money, which is 413.7M, comes from the Hillary Clinton Campaign, and about 71M comes from small donations of $200 or less. (Washington Post)
